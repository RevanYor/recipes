Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

How to make a Sandwich

  1. Open the Bread
  2. Put the Ham on the Bread
  3. Put the Cheese on the Bread
  4. Put the Lettuce on the Bread
  5. Put the Tomato on the Bread
  6. Close the Bread
  7. Enjoy

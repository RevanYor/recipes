Concentrated dark Matter

Description

The special fuel to travel through space faster than anybody else!

Ingredients:

  • Cesium
  • Plutonic Quarks
  • Bottled water
  • A bucket

How to make Concentrated Dark Matter

  1. Two parts Plutonic Quarks into the bucket.
  2. One part Cesium into the bucket.
  3. Empty the water bottle into the bucket.
  4. Pour it all into the fuel tank, so you can go wherever you want.
